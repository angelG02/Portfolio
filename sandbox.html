Sunset Engine Showcase

DISCLAIMER! Needs to be opened in a browser that supports WebGPU (Edge or Chrome are the ones I know of). If nothing shows under this, then please open in Edge or Chrome. Also, the game runs on a self-hoted server, so please contact me if you would like to try it out for yourself!

Controls

  • Mouse wheel - increase/decrease camera movement speed
  • Hold LMB and move up/down - move camera forward/back
  • Hold RMB and move left/right - Rotate model around Y axis
